Usually it is difficult to remember the prescription as patients find it hard to memorize the names of tablet pills or medicine.
Taking medicine on time is very important for patient inorder to recover to good health.
When a patient is suffering from more than two diseases it becomes a tedious job to follow the prescription.
So, let us consider an instance where a patient is suffering from Heart problem, diabetes and Blood pressure problems and each disease has three different medicine to be taken on three different times.
We are trying to develop a web app which eases the patient by altering him/her with proper medicine name and it's schedule.
